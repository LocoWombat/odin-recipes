Macaroni and Cheese

Macaroni and Cheese is one of my favorite dishes to make. It's incredibly simple and filling, with kids and adults alike able to enjoy the flavor.

Are you ready to dig in?

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of Pasta
  • Half a stick of butter
  • A bunch of shredded cheddar
  • A bunch of shredded gruyere
  • Whatever other shredded cheese you want
  • A cup of full-fat milk
  • A cup of heavy cream

Steps

  1. Boil a pot of heavily-salted water and let your pasta cook for about 8 minutes
  2. In a separate pot, melt the butter and begin mixing in about a tablespoon of flour to create your roux
  3. Once the roux is made, slowly add in the milk and heavy cream
  4. Begin putting your cheeses in a handful at a time, making sure to stir regularly
  5. Season as desired with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and paprika
  6. Drain the pasta and transfer it to a baking dish
  7. Add the cheese sauce, and top with shredded cheddar
  8. Bake for 20 minutes on 425. Enjoy!
